Priyanto Soedarsono

Managing Partner

Priyanto has almost 20-year experience in capital market industry. He has the last position as Chief Investment Officer and Director at PT Principal Asset Management (Principal) from Nov 2016 to May 2020. Prior to Principal, he spent 5 years at PT Danareksa Investment Management with the last position as Head of Investment from 2011 to 2016. He was in charge as Head of Fixed Income at PT CIMB Principal Asset Management for 3 years from 2008 to 2011. Prior to that he was an Investment Manager at PT Mandiri Manajemen Investasi and Research Analyst at PT Mandiri Sekuritas.

Nene Harmulyo

Managing Partner

Nene has more than 22 years experience in the capital market industry, which mainly manages Sharia products. He has held several positions in the product development, operational and compliance division in several companies including Batavia Prosperindo Asset Management, Manulife Asset Management, BNP Paribas IP and Principal Asset Management. Nene graduated from a sharia master degree at The Tazkia School of Islamic Economics. He holds an Investment Manager license from OJK and a Capital Market Sharia Expert certificate from Ticmi. Before joining INPARTNER, he was Former Director and Head of the Sharia Investment Unit at Majoris Asset Management.
